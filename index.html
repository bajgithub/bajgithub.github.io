Now this has branched into a site for some musings as well, so I've haphazardly combined the two and you can go down the rabbit hole that you please.

Some of my portfolio is still a work in progress, both in in terms of web development and rounding up all of the projects (and their respective descriptions and photos) I have done in my time as an undergrad; all I ask for is a little mercy and patience.
